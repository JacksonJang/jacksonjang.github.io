FoodMemo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2024

1. Purpose and Types of Personal Information Collected

FoodMemo collects the following personal information to help users record and manage their daily meals:

  • Photos: To document meal images
  • Title: To provide a title for meal records
  • Content: To write notes and details about meals
  • Meal Time: To record when the meal was consumed
  • Portion Size: To note whether the portion was appropriate

2. Methods of Collecting Personal Information

FoodMemo collects personal information directly from users. This includes uploading photos, entering titles and content, selecting meal times, and writing notes on portion sizes through the app.

3. Use of Personal Information

The collected personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To record and manage users' meal information
  • To provide personalized meal analysis and feedback
  • To improve app functionality and enhance user experience

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

FoodMemo retains and uses users' personal information for the duration of the app service. If a user requests account deletion or data removal, we will promptly delete the information.

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

FoodMemo does not share users' personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

6. Measures to Ensure the Security of Personal Information

FoodMemo takes the following measures to ensure the security of users' personal information:

  • Data encryption
  • Access control management
  • Installation and regular inspection of security programs

7. Users' Rights

Users can view or modify their personal information at any time and can request the deletion of their information. Users can access these options through the app settings menu or by contacting customer support.
